Our journey so far
Created in 2001, Emakina was born from the merger of three agencies with complementary expertise: Emalaya, NetAtWork and Ex Machina. In 2006, it entered Alternext, Euronext’s segment for growing SMEs.
This splash into the financial markets enabled our expansion, which started in 2007 with the acquisition of Belgian agencies The Reference, Design is Dead and Your Agency, which have been part of our group ever since. We’ve spread across Europe, into the Netherlands, France, Switzerland, Turkey, Austria, Sweden, Croatia, Serbia and Poland. The group has also settled into the Middle East in Beirut, as well as into Africa with our Cape Town office.
Emakina has become the leading digital agency in Belgium and one of the top 20 players in Europe. Since joining EPAM in 2021, our capabilities are boosted and strengthened by digital transformation and product engineering expertise. We are fusing creativity, strategy, engineering and analytics to best serve our clients.
